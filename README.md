NOTES:
Technique used is dividing by all odd or even numbers up to square root of given number.

Required calculations to decide if number is prime is as follows:
under 25 (5^2) - 1
under 49 (7^2) - 2
under 81 (9^2) - 3
under 121(11^2)- 4
